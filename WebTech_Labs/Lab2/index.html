Sports Week 2025

Sports Week is one of the most vibrant and eagerly awaited events in our college calendar. It goes beyond being just a series of games, it is a celebration of energy, teamwork, and the spirit of healthy competition. Throughout the week, students from different faculties come together to participate in a wide range of sports, from athletics and football to indoor games, showcasing not only their skills but also their determination and discipline. The event provides a refreshing break from academic routines, encouraging students to embrace fitness, build resilience, and strengthen bonds with their peers. More than winning or losing, Sports Week is about fostering unity, school spirit, and lifelong memories. It reminds us that sports are not only about physical strength but also about character, cooperation, and the joy of shared achievement, making it an essential and enriching part of campus life.
